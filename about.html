About Us

Our Mission

At Cafe Cesura, our mission is to consistently provide quality food and beverages, using fresh ingredients from local and environmentally sustainable suppliers when possible, and to be a good neighbor to Downtown Bellevue and the larger community. We were founded in 2010 by identical triplets - Mike, Mark and Max.
